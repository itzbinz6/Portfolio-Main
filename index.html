FAQ

Common questions

What are you actually working on right now?
A few things at once — always. Web projects for clients, a blockchain development path I'm working through on my own, and quietly getting better at prompt engineering. I also have some personal projects I'm not ready to talk about yet. They're early. They're mine.
What do you do when you're not in front of a screen?
Watching anime, mostly. I go for the ones that make you think — Attack on Titan, Summertime Rendering, Shangri-La Frontier. Outside of that, music. Gospel when I need to feel something, African praise medleys when I need energy. Dunsin Oyekan and Kaestrings are on heavy rotation.
How do you actually work — what's your style?
I think before I move. Detail matters a lot to me — I'll spend more time than necessary on something small if it doesn't feel right. I'm not the fastest, but I'm thorough. And I try to be honest about what I know and what I'm still figuring out.
What's something most people don't know about you?
I'm genuinely fun to be around. The portfolio makes me look serious — and I am about the work — but I don't take myself too seriously. Also, No Game No Life deserves a season 2 and I will die on that hill.
What's the best way to reach you?
WhatsApp is fastest. Email works too. I read everything and reply to most things. If you just want to say something — a thought, a question, feedback on something you saw here — that's welcome too. No agenda needed.
