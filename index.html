FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to build a landing page?
Most landing pages take between 3–7 days depending on complexity. Single-service pages are faster; pages with multiple sections, a blog, or custom animations take closer to a week. I'll give you a realistic timeline before we start.
Do you handle hosting and deployment?
Yes. I can deploy your site to Netlify (free tier works for most small businesses) or help you connect a custom domain. I'll walk you through everything so you know how it works.
What information do you need to get started?
Business name, what you sell or offer, your contact details (especially WhatsApp), any photos or logos you have, and your preferred tone — casual or formal. We can work without some of these if needed.
Can I see examples before I pay?
Absolutely — that's what the Projects section above is for. Every demo is live and fully functional. You can browse them on your phone or laptop to get a feel for the quality.
Do you accept payment in Naira?
Yes, I do. We can discuss pricing and payment terms over WhatsApp before any work starts. No surprises.
